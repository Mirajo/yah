Targeted Audience

Yah's Cuisine target audience is the surrounding community of South Shore residents who have a desire to eat healthy, hearty, and tasty meals. This community is predominately African American with a population of 52,990. The average resident is 33 year old single mothers with a median income of 28,943.00.
